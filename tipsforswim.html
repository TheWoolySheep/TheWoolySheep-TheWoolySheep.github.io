Tips for Swimming

I love swimming. It's been one of my main enjoyments in life. I like to joke that if I came back as an animal I would be an otter. I love it! As a competitive swimmer, I want to give what I think is the most important piece of advice for each stroke.

Butterfly Backstroke Breaststroke Freestyle
Keep your undulation smooth and continuous. Keep your head back and keep kicking. Shoot forward...fast! Be wise with your breathing. Less for sprints, more for distance.
